# Events playbook

Formats and norms for Civic House events.

## Formats

- **Meetup** — low-key gathering; networking, updates, mutual support.
- **Info session** — one topic (rights, resources, process); no legal advice; signpost only.
- **Workshop** — skills or civic literacy; facilitator-led; materials shared when possible.
- **Round table** — small group; discussion; Chatham House or agreed rules.

## Norms

- Start and end on time.
- Safety and conduct policy applies (see safety_and_conduct.md).
- No representation claims; no sovereign or government symbolism that could mislead.
- Record only what participants consent to; no covert recording.

## Checklist (before event)

- Venue confirmed; safety and access clear.
- One responsible lead; contact visible.
- Topic and format communicated in advance.
