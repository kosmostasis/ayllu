# Service catalog v0

What a Civic House node can offer. Expand per local need and capacity.

## Credentialing & verification

- Skills verification (attestations; link to credential primitives when defined).
- Participation history (events, contributions).
- Contributions ledger (who gave what; dispute resolution if needed).

## Opportunities

- Job and opportunity board (curated; host-nation compliant).
- Referrals to legal, housing, education resources (no legal advice; signposting only).

## Mutual aid

- Coordination for mutual aid (time, skills, small funds) within node and network rules.
- Clear boundaries: no representation claims; host-law compliant.

## Learning

- Workshops and learning sessions (language, skills, civic literacy).
- Formats per events_playbook.md.

## Events

- Regular meetups, info sessions, and formats from events_playbook.md.

---

*All services are complementary to host-state structures and compliant with local law.*
