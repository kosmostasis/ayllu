# Civic House — brand

**Name / positioning:** Diaspora civic infrastructure for Venezuelans + allies + future citizens. Services, credentials, coordination.

**Tone:** Practical, inclusive, host-nation respectful. No representation claims.

**Do:**
- Complement host-state structures.
- Offer concrete value (credentialing, opportunities, mutual aid, learning).
- Be explicit about constraints (complementary posture, host-nation compliant).

**Don’t:**
- Claim to represent Venezuela or any government.
- Operate in conflict with host-country law or norms.
- Use language that implies sovereign or diplomatic status.
