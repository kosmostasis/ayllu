# Dispute resolution (template)

- **Tiers:** (1) Informal — direct conversation, lead can facilitate. (2) Mediation — neutral mediator, voluntary. (3) Escalation — document, follow host-country law.
- **Principles:** Confidentiality where safe; no retaliation for good-faith reporting.
- **Who mediates:** Node designates non-party mediators; network may maintain list when protocol exists.
